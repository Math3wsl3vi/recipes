Banana pancakes

Pancakes are a type of flat, thin, and round cake made from a batter that usually
consists of flour, milk, eggs, and a leavening agent such as baking powder or
baking soda. Pancakes are a popular breakfast item in many cultures around
the world and are often served with a variety of toppings such as syrup, butter,
fruits, whipped cream, or chocolate chips.

Prep time

5 mins.

Cook time

3 mins.

Servings

5 people.

Instructions

STEP 1:Combine Dry Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder,
baking soda, and salt.

STEP 2:Prepare Wet Ingredients

In a separate bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork. Add the
buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Mix well
until the wet ingredients are well combined

STEP 3:Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients

Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until
just combined. Be careful not to overmix; it's okay if there are a
few lumps

STEP 4:Preheat the Pan

Preheat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat. You can test
if it's hot enough by sprinkling a few drops of water on the surface; if
they sizzle and evaporate, the pan is ready.

STEP 5:Cook the Pancakes

Grease the pan with butter or cooking spray. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for
each pancake onto the griddle. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, and
the edges start to look set, usually 2-3 minutes.

STEP 6:Flip and Cook the Other Side

Flip the pancakes with a spatula and cook the other side for an additional
1-2 minutes or until golden brown.

STEP 7: Serve

Transfer the pancakes to a plate and repeat the process with the remaining batter.
Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings such as sliced bananas,
maple syrup, chopped nuts, or whipped cream

Ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour

1 tablespoon granulated sugar

1 teaspoon baking powder

Tools

Hand Blender

Measuring Spoons
